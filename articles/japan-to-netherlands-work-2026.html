30% Ruling — Major Tax Benefit

The Netherlands' famous "30% ruling" allows qualifying expat workers to receive 30% of their gross salary tax-free for up to 5 years. This significantly increases take-home pay for high-earning HSM permit holders. Japanese nationals with a job offer generally qualify automatically.

Orientation Year Visa

Japanese nationals who recently completed a degree at a Dutch university, or who graduated from a top-100 world university (which includes several Japanese universities: Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka), can apply for the Orientation Year visa (Zoekjaar) to spend 1 year job-hunting in the Netherlands.
