The UAE — and Dubai in particular — has attracted growing numbers of Japanese professionals in recent years. Zero income tax, world-class infrastructure, and proximity to Japan (9-hour direct flight) make it an increasingly popular escape from Japan's deflation, high taxes, and intense work culture. Japanese nationals are highly regarded in the UAE for precision, punctuality, and technical skills.

UAE Work Visa Process for Japanese

  1. Secure a job offer from a UAE-registered company
  2. Employer submits employment visa application to MOHRE (Ministry of Human Resources)
  3. Medical fitness test in UAE after arrival on entry permit
  4. Emirates ID biometrics registration
  5. Receive residence visa stamp in passport (2-year validity, renewable)

Japanese nationals can enter UAE visa-free for 30 days for job search purposes.

Golden Visa — Long-Term UAE Residency

For highly skilled professionals, the UAE Golden Visa offers 10-year residency without employer sponsorship requirements. Japanese nationals can qualify through:

  • Salary of AED 30,000+ per month as a specialist
  • Investment in UAE real estate (AED 2 million+)
  • Outstanding academic credentials or professional achievement
