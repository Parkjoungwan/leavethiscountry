Thailand is one of the easiest international destinations for Indian passport holders in 2026. Indians can enter on a Visa on Arrival (VoA) or use the Thailand e-Visa system — both options are quick, affordable, and allow stays of up to 30 days. With budget-friendly food, accommodation, and activities, Thailand remains Southeast Asia's most popular destination for Indian travelers.

Recommended: The Thailand e-Visa is the most convenient option — apply online 3–5 days before travel and skip the VoA queue at the airport. The 30-day stay is also double that of VoA.

Thailand e-Visa Application Process

  1. Apply at the official Thailand e-Visa portal (evisa.thaievisa.go.th)
  2. Upload your passport copy, photo, and flight/hotel details
  3. Pay the fee online by credit/debit card
  4. Receive approval by email within 3–5 business days
  5. Print or show the e-Visa on your phone at the port of entry

Popular Destinations for Indian Travelers

  • Bangkok — temples, malls, street food, nightlife
  • Phuket — beaches, water sports, island hopping
  • Pattaya — large Indian community, beach resort city
  • Chiang Mai — hill temples, night markets, cooler climate
  • Krabi / Koh Samui — islands, luxury resorts

Tips for Indian Travelers

  • Thailand has a massive Indian diaspora — vegetarian/Indian food is easy to find in major tourist areas
  • Respect local customs: dress modestly at temples, remove shoes when entering
  • Carry enough cash (Thai Baht) — many smaller vendors don't accept cards
  • Get travel insurance — Thai hospitals are excellent but can be expensive for foreigners
  • Avoid buying from tuk-tuk drivers who offer "city tours" — these often lead to commission shops
