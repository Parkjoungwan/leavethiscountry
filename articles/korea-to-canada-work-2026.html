Korean citizens have one of the best work pathways to Canada globally, thanks to the IEC (International Experience Canada) agreement between Korea and Canada. If you're under 35, you can get an open work permit — no job offer needed — and work for any Canadian employer for up to 24 months. This is the fastest, cheapest route to working in Canada.

Route 1: IEC Working Holiday (Best for Under 35)

The IEC Working Holiday is a lottery-based open work permit with no restrictions on employer or industry:

  • Age: 18–35 (must be under 35 on day of application)
  • Fee: CAD $156 (work permit) + $100 biometrics
  • Duration: Up to 24 months
  • Work type: Any — you don't need a job offer
  • Process: Submit profile online → invited by lottery → accept invitation → apply for permit

IEC draws happen throughout the year. Korean applicants are typically drawn quickly as the Korean quota is one of Canada's largest bilateral agreements.

Route 2: Express Entry (Best for Permanent Career Path)

For professionals seeking skilled work with a path to PR, Express Entry is the standard route:

Korean applicants typically score well in Express Entry due to strong education systems and English proficiency. Many Koreans use the IEC first, gain Canadian work experience, then transition to Express Entry CEC for PR.

The IEC → Express Entry Pipeline

  1. Apply for IEC Working Holiday (under 35)
  2. Work in Canada for 12+ months in a NOC TEER 0/1/2/3 occupation
  3. Create Express Entry profile with Canadian work experience
  4. Receive Invitation to Apply (ITA) for PR
  5. Submit PR application — approved in 6 months
  6. Become a Canadian Permanent Resident

This pipeline typically takes 2.5–3.5 years from IEC arrival to PR — one of the fastest PR pathways globally.

Key Documents for IEC Application

  • Valid Korean passport (must be valid throughout permit duration)
  • Proof of funds: CAD $2,500 minimum recommended
  • Round-trip ticket or proof you can purchase one
  • Travel insurance for the full duration
  • No criminal record
