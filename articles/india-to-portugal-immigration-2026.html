The D7 Visa: Most Popular Route for Indians

The D7 is Portugal's "passive income" residency visa — designed for retirees, remote workers, freelancers, and investors who can prove a stable monthly income without needing local employment.

D7 Requirements

  • Proof of passive income ≥ €1,020/month (Portuguese minimum wage)
  • Clean criminal background check from India (apostilled)
  • Proof of accommodation in Portugal (rental contract or property deed)
  • Health insurance valid in Portugal
  • Bank statements showing financial stability

Step-by-Step D7 Application Process

  1. Gather documents: income proof, bank statements, criminal record, accommodation
  2. Apply for D7 visa at the Portuguese consulate in India (Mumbai or New Delhi)
  3. Receive 4-month entry visa
  4. Travel to Portugal and schedule AIMA (immigration authority) appointment
  5. Obtain 2-year residence permit (renewable)
  6. After 5 years: apply for Permanent Residency or Citizenship

Path to EU Citizenship

Portugal offers one of the fastest paths to European citizenship among EU countries:

  • 5 years of legal residency → eligible for Permanent Residency or Citizenship
  • Portuguese citizenship grants you an EU passport with visa-free access to 185+ countries
  • Portugal allows dual citizenship — you can keep your Indian passport
  • Language requirement: basic Portuguese (A2 level) for citizenship

Cost of Living in Portugal

  • Lisbon: €1,500–€2,500/month (rent + living)
  • Porto: €1,200–€2,000/month
  • Interior towns (Braga, Évora, Coimbra): €800–€1,400/month
  • Healthcare: public system available to residents, or private at €30–€80/consultation
