How the Employment Pass Works

The EP is the standard route for most Korean professionals. The application is submitted by the employer, not the employee.

  1. Secure a job offer from a Singapore employer
  2. Employer submits EP application via MOM's EP Online
  3. MOM assesses using COMPASS (Complementarity Assessment Framework) — points-based scoring
  4. Processing: 3–8 weeks (can use EP eService for faster decisions)
  5. Collect EP card on arrival in Singapore
  6. Dependant passes available for spouse and children

Singapore Job Market for Koreans

  • Finance: Banking, wealth management, fintech — strong demand in Raffles Place / Marina Bay
  • Technology: Regional HQs of Google, Meta, Grab, Sea Group all hire in Singapore
  • Manufacturing: Samsung, SK Hynix, and TSMC have significant Singapore operations
  • Korean language roles: Translator, business development for Korea-Singapore trade
  • Average Software Engineer salary: SGD $6,000–$12,000/month

Cost of Living in Singapore

  • Rent (1BR in central area): SGD $2,500–$4,000/month
  • Korean food widely available in Tanjong Pagar and Orchard areas
  • No income tax below SGD $20,000; progressive rates above (top rate 24%)
  • CPF (Central Provident Fund): mandatory savings scheme — 20% employee contribution
