Canada has historically been the #1 destination for Hong Kong emigrants — the 1990s wave before the 1997 handover created massive HK diaspora communities in Vancouver and Toronto. Canada maintains this tradition: a series of HK-specific immigration measures launched in 2021 made it easier than ever for Hong Kongers to enter, work, and obtain permanent residency in Canada.

HK-Specific Immigration Streams (2021–2026)

  • Open Work Permit for HK residents: HKSAR passport holders can get 3-year open work permits allowing any employment
  • HK University Graduates: Recent graduates from HK institutions get priority processing and lower bar to PR
  • Public Policy streams: IRCC (Immigration Canada) regularly adds HK-specific pathways — check the IRCC website for current openings

HK Advantage in Express Entry

  • Most HK residents are educated in English — IELTS scores tend to be high (CLB 9–10 = significant CRS points)
  • Many HK professionals have degrees from Hong Kong's world-class universities (HKU, HKUST, CUHK)
  • Finance, engineering, IT — all high-demand NOCs in Canada
  • Canadian work experience from WHV or study permit is the fastest CEC route

Where Hong Kongers Settle in Canada

  • Metro Vancouver / Richmond: "Hongcouver" — the largest HK expat community outside HK; Cantonese is the third-most spoken language in Richmond
  • Toronto / Markham / Scarborough: major Cantonese-speaking communities, HK-style restaurants, dim sum, Chinese newspapers
  • Calgary: growing HK community, lower cost of living, oil industry jobs
