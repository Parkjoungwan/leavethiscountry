Italy is one of the most popular European destinations for Korean travelers, and South Korean passport holders enjoy visa-free access to Italy and the entire Schengen zone for up to 90 days in any 180-day period. Italy's rich history, art, food, and fashion make it a natural bucket-list destination — and a growing Korean presence in Milan and Rome means the experience is increasingly Korean-friendly.

Entry Requirements for Korean Travelers

  • Valid South Korean passport
  • ETIAS travel authorization (€7, online — check current rollout status)
  • Return flight booking
  • Accommodation bookings
  • No visa application, no consulate visit required

Must-Visit Destinations

  • Rome — Colosseum, Vatican, Trevi Fountain, Roman Forum
  • Florence — Uffizi Gallery, Duomo, Michelangelo's David
  • Venice — canals, gondolas, St Mark's Basilica (avoid summer crowds)
  • Milan — fashion capital, Leonardo's Last Supper, Lake Como day trip
  • Amalfi Coast — Positano, Ravello, dramatic coastal scenery
  • Cinque Terre — five colorful coastal villages; hiking trails between them

Italian Food Guide for Korean Travelers

  • Authentic pizza is Neapolitan (Naples/Rome) — thin crust, simple toppings
  • Gelato: avoid tourist traps (piled-high, colorful gelato is often low quality) — look for "artigianale" signs
  • Coffee culture: espresso standing at the bar costs €1–€1.50; sitting at a table costs 2–3x more
  • Vegetarian options are widely available — Italy's pasta and pizza culture is naturally vegetable-forward

Tips for Korean Travelers

  • Book Colosseum and Vatican tickets at least 2 weeks in advance — online only, no walk-up tickets
  • Watch for pickpockets on Rome's Metro Line A and around tourist sites
  • Tap water in Italy is safe and good quality — free refills at public drinking fountains (nasoni)
  • Korea-Italy cultural events happen regularly in Milan and Rome through the Korean Cultural Centre
  • Travel between cities by Trenitalia Frecciarossa (high-speed) — much faster and more convenient than flying
