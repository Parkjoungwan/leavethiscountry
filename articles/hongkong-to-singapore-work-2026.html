Singapore and Hong Kong have long been Asia's two dominant financial centers — and since 2021, Singapore has increasingly been the destination of choice for Hong Kong finance professionals and family offices seeking political stability while remaining in Asia. The cultural familiarity (Cantonese-speaking community, Chinese food culture, common Confucian social values) makes the move less jarring than moving to the West.

Why HK Professionals Choose Singapore

  • Common ground: both are financial hubs, English-speaking, Chinese-majority, with similar legal systems inherited from British colonial period
  • Family offices: Singapore actively courted HK family offices with tax incentives — many ultra-high-net-worth HK families have relocated their family office operations to Singapore
  • Flight time: 3.5 hours from Hong Kong — convenient for maintaining HK business connections
  • Stable government: Singapore's political stability is a key draw for HK professionals seeking certainty
  • Cantonese community: significant Cantonese-speaking population in Singapore (Teochew and Hokkien communities also overlap culturally)

Employment Pass Requirements

  • Job offer from Singapore-registered employer (sponsor)
  • Minimum salary: SGD $5,000/month (higher for financial services: SGD $5,500+)
  • COMPASS assessment: points scored on salary percentile, educational qualification, nationality diversity, skills bonus
  • Processing: 3–8 weeks via MOM EP Online
  • Initial 2-year permit, renewable for 3 years
