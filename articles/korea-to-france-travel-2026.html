South Korean passport holders enjoy visa-free access to France and the entire Schengen Area for up to 90 days within any 180-day period. No advance application, no consulate visit, no waiting — simply register for ETIAS (if required) and fly. France is one of the world's most visited countries, and Paris in particular has a significant Korean cultural presence, from K-beauty stores to Korean restaurants in the Marais and near Saint-Germain.

Entry Requirements for Korean Travelers

  • Valid South Korean passport (recommended 3+ months validity beyond travel dates)
  • ETIAS authorization: the EU's new travel authorization system (€7, valid 3 years) — check current status as rollout has been phased
  • Return/onward flight ticket may be requested
  • Proof of accommodation (hotel bookings)
  • Proof of sufficient funds (€100/day per EU guideline)

Important: The 90-day limit applies to the entire Schengen area — days spent in Germany, Spain, Italy, etc. all count toward your 90-day France allowance. Plan multi-country European trips accordingly.

Korea–France: A Cultural Connection

France and South Korea have a uniquely close cultural relationship. Korean art, cinema, and food have strong followings in France, and the Korean community in Paris is established and visible:

  • Paris Korea Town: around the 13th arrondissement (Avenue de Choisy) and the Opera area
  • BTS, K-drama, and K-pop events frequently happen in Paris and other French cities
  • Korean food stores and restaurants widely available in Paris
  • The Louvre, Palace of Versailles, and Eiffel Tower are popular Korean traveler spots

Tips for Korean Travelers to France

  • Book Louvre tickets online in advance — same-day entry is often unavailable
  • Pickpocketing is common at tourist sites — use a money belt or anti-theft bag
  • Many Parisian restaurants close between 2:30–7:30pm; plan meals around this
  • The Paris Metro is efficient — get a Navigo weekly pass for unlimited travel (€30/week)
  • Direct flights: Korean Air and Air France operate Incheon–CDG daily
