The UAE — particularly Dubai and Abu Dhabi — has become a major destination for Korean professionals seeking tax-free income in a dynamic, cosmopolitan environment. South Korea and the UAE have strong economic ties through construction, engineering, energy, and finance sectors. Korean passport holders also enjoy visa-free entry to the UAE for 90 days, making job searches convenient before committing to work sponsorship.

UAE Work Visa Process for Koreans

The UAE does not have a self-sponsored work visa system in the traditional sense — most foreign workers obtain employment through an employer-sponsored route:

  1. Secure a job offer from a UAE employer
  2. Employer applies for an Entry Permit (work permit) with MOHRE or GDRFA
  3. Receive Entry Permit via email (~2 weeks)
  4. Travel to UAE on Entry Permit
  5. Complete medical fitness test and Emirates ID biometrics in UAE
  6. Receive Residence Visa (2–3 years) stamped in passport

Alternative: UAE Golden Visa

The UAE's Golden Visa offers long-term residency (5–10 years) without employer sponsorship for:

  • Investors (real estate AED 2M+, or business investment)
  • Entrepreneurs with approved startup or AED 500K investment
  • Specialized talent: doctors, engineers, scientists, artists, athletes
  • Outstanding students (GPA 3.8+ from top universities)
