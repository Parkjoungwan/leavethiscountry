Spain is emerging as a study destination for Indian students seeking affordable European education. Public universities like Universidad Complutense de Madrid and Universidad de Barcelona offer quality degrees at a fraction of UK or US costs. Spain's relaxed lifestyle, warm climate, and growing startup scene in Madrid and Barcelona make it an increasingly popular choice.

Spanish Student Visa Application Process

  1. Get admission from a Spanish university or language school
  2. Prepare required documents (see below)
  3. Book appointment at Spanish consulate in India (Mumbai, New Delhi, or Chennai)
  4. Submit application with €80 fee
  5. Biometrics taken at consulate
  6. Visa issued in 4–8 weeks
  7. Upon arrival in Spain: apply for TIE (Tarjeta de Identidad de Extranjero) residence card within 30 days

Documents Required

  • Passport (valid 6+ months beyond intended stay)
  • University acceptance letter
  • Proof of funds (€6,000–€8,000 for first year)
  • Private health insurance (not relying on public system)
  • Criminal background check (apostilled)
  • Academic transcripts and degree certificates

Post-Study Options

  • Job search authorization: after completing studies, students can apply for a 12-month authorization to look for work
  • Digital Nomad Visa: if working remotely for a non-Spanish company, Spain's Digital Nomad Visa (€73 fee) allows long-term stays
  • Spain's startup ecosystem is growing — particularly in Barcelona (tech) and Madrid (fintech)

Key Tips for Indian Students

  • Apostille your Indian documents through the Ministry of External Affairs before applying
  • Most undergraduate programs are in Spanish — look for English-taught master's programs
  • Salamanca, Seville, and Valencia are cheaper alternatives to Madrid and Barcelona
  • NIE (Número de Identidad de Extranjero) is needed for banking and contracts — apply at police station soon after arriving
  • IESE, IE Business School, and ESADE are world-class Spanish business schools with English MBA programs
