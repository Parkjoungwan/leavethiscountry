PASSPORT TYPE MATTERS

• HKSAR passport holders: Must apply for a Schengen visa at Italian consulate or VFS (€80, allow 4–6 weeks)
• BN(O) passport holders: Enter Italy and the entire Schengen Area visa-free for 90 days — no appointment needed
If you hold both, use BN(O) for European travel.

HKSAR: Schengen Visa for Italy

  • Fee: €80 (adults), €40 (ages 6–11), €0 (under 6)
  • Apply: Italian consulate HK or VFS Global Italy
  • Processing: 15–30 days (apply 6+ weeks in advance)
  • Documents needed: passport, 2 passport photos, travel insurance (€30,000 min), round-trip flight booking, hotel bookings, 3-month bank statements, employment letter or proof of funds
  • Validity: Schengen visa covers all 27 Schengen countries — use it to visit France, Spain, etc. on same trip

Italy's Pull for HK Visitors

  • Italy is consistently one of Hong Kong's most popular European long-haul destinations
  • Fashion: Milan is a pilgrimage for HK fashion enthusiasts — Prada, Gucci, Versace, Armani flagships
  • Food: Italian cuisine — especially pasta, pizza, gelato, espresso — is hugely popular in HK
  • History and art: Rome, Florence, Venice offer world-class cultural experiences
  • Cathay Pacific operates direct flights HK–Milan and HK–Rome

Must-Visit for HK Travelers

  • Rome — Vatican Museums, Colosseum, Pantheon, Campo de' Fiori; 3+ days minimum
  • Florence — Uffizi Gallery (book months ahead!), Duomo, Ponte Vecchio leather market
  • Venice — unique; best visited shoulder season (spring/autumn) to avoid summer crowds
  • Milan — fashion shopping, Da Vinci's Last Supper (book way ahead), La Scala opera house
  • Amalfi Coast — best April–June, September–October; Positano, Ravello, Capri by ferry
