About LeaveThisCountry

LeaveThisCountry is a free visa comparison tool that answers one question: where is it easiest — and cheapest — to go from your country?

Unlike traditional visa checker tools that only tell you whether you're eligible, LeaveThisCountry ranks destinations by cost, difficulty, and processing time — so you can compare your options at a glance.

What we compare

  • Cost — estimated annual cost in USD including visa fees and living expenses
  • Difficulty — Easy / Moderate / Hard based on rejection rates, document complexity, and language requirements
  • Processing time — realistic wait times, not just official minimums
  • Key conditions — the one thing you must know before applying

Who is this for?

  • Students researching affordable study destinations
  • Skilled workers exploring job visa options
  • Families planning permanent relocation
  • Frequent travelers wanting visa-free access

Current coverage

We currently support departures from India and South Korea, with comparisons across 8 popular destinations: Canada, Germany, Australia, Singapore, UAE, UK, Japan, and Portugal.

We are actively expanding to cover more origin countries including Philippines, Bangladesh, Nigeria, and Brazil.

Editorial Standards

All data is manually researched and verified against official government immigration portals. Sources include:

  • IRCC Canada (canada.ca)
  • Make it in Germany (make-it-in-germany.com)
  • Australian Department of Home Affairs (homeaffairs.gov.au)
  • ICA Singapore (ica.gov.sg)
  • UAE Government Portal (u.ae)
  • UK Home Office (gov.uk)
  • Japan Immigration (moj.go.jp)
